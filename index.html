Sid's Suits is a local suit store located in London, Ontario. Our number one mission is keep you looking SNAZZY! We offer a wide range of suits and accessories all made with the highest of quality. Our experts are all highly knowledgable and will ensure you leave the store feeling satisfied, confident, and looking amazing.

Meet The Team

Sid Koyo
CEO

Sid Koyo is a london local who got into the fashion and retail industry after realizing his true passion of formal fashion after taking a fashion course in his twelth grade. Using money from his part time job at a car wash, he's put together a wondersful establishment here at Sid's Suits.

Sarah Mayne
Stylist

Sarah has completed a degree in Fashion from the University of Toronto. As a Toronto native, Sarah has moved to London, Ontario to be one of the revolutionary stylists in the city and grow her brand. She offers a wide range of styling expertise and will have you feeling like a model.

Andras Clauding
Accounting and Tailor

Andras is another London Local who completed his accounting degree at Western University. He has always had a passion for numbers and wants to use his degree to help small businesses. His dad was also very much into sewing and since has taught him the art of tailoring which is included in any purchase from our store!
